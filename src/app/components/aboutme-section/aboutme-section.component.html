About me

“It’s been amazing working with him! I whish I could just replace all my coworkers with copies of him”, “Great Software Engineer, better person.”, “He solves every problem so quickly! I could watch him solve problems all day”… no one ever said these, but this is what my past coworkers probably thought of me had you asked them.

I consider myself a Software Engineer. Not a frontend{{' '}}/{{' '}}backend{{' '}}/{{' '}}fullstack{{' '}}/{{' '}}whatever developer, but an Engineer. With time I’ve come to realize that what makes a good engineer is not how many tools or languages he/she masters, but his/her ability to solve problems, and that’s what I do and (more importantly) enjoy most.

I’m a curious and adventurous person, and so I started this career thinking of the many doors it could open and the many paths I could take, and the possibilities to always learn new stuff and travel the world.

I started my career in the back as that was the focus of my university degree, covid struck when I had barely landed in France for work and so I took that time to learn more of the front, working voluntarily for Rescue App and as a freelance building websites. Then I worked for Kokopilot, studied Art History at University of Malaga for 1.5 years (I love art, philosophy, history!... but had to focus on my career and keep self-learning those subjects I love) and ended in A2Systems, sharpening my front-end skills and discovering a fondness for design as well.

I've always loved creating. I used to draw when I was a kid, then started making music, and now I entertain myself building webs like this. I also like challenges and solving problems (with the invaluable help of coffe, google and AI). So if you need a beautiful software engineer to engineer your software, I might be your man.
